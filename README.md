# Business Sales Performance Analytics
## Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales performance using Power BI to identify revenue trends, profitable categories, and high-performing regions.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## KPIs Analyzed
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Revenue YoY %
- Revenue by Category
- Profit by Region
- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Top Selling Products

## Key Insights
• The business generated $2.3M in total revenue with $286.4K total profit.  
• Profit margin is 12.47%, indicating stable profitability.  
• Revenue shows strong growth with 46.88% year-over-year growth.  
• Technology is the highest revenue and profit generating category.  
• West region generates the highest revenue among all regions.

## Business Recommendations
• Increase focus on the Technology category to maximize profit.  
• Improve cost control in the Furniture category to increase margins.  
• Expand strategies from the West region into other regions.  
• Focus marketing during high-performing months.

## Dashboard
The Power BI dashboard visualizes:
- Revenue trends over time
- Category-wise revenue
- Regional revenue performance
- Monthly revenue patterns
- Top performing products
